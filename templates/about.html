About me

I'm Anmol Sriwastava. Currently, a Statistical Data Science student at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata.

My interest sits at the intersection of ML, AI and Data, complemented with mathematical rigour. Not just running models but actually understanding what is happening underneath and using those foundations to build things that work.

I do not want to spend my time only inside academics and theory. Theory matters, but it should lead somewhere. I want to build tools, systems and machines.

This site is where I put things I learn and build. Notes, project writeups, and writing on topics I find interesting. I write about Algorithms, Machine Learning, Applied AI and AI Research, and occasionally robotics and IoT when something worth writing about comes up as I spent a good chunk of time with Arduino, sensors and circuits in my school and built a lot of small robotics and IoT projects,and learned more from that than from most formal coursework.

Always open to collaborating, contributing or just talking about something interesting.
